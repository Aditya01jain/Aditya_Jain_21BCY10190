Game Rules

1. Each player controls pieces labeled 'A' or 'B'.

2. The objective is to move your pieces strategically to capture the opponent's pieces or reach the end of the board.

3. 'P' pieces move left, right, up, or down.

4. 'H1' and 'H2' pieces can move in more complex patterns like diagonals or forward jumps.

5. The game ends when one player captures all of the opponent's key pieces or meets the winning criteria.
