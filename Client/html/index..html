Game Rules

  1. Each player controls pieces labeled 'A' or 'B'.
  2. The objective is to move your pieces strategically to capture the opponent's pieces or reach the end of the board.
  3. 'P' pieces can move left, right, up, or down by one step.
  4. 'H1' pieces can move two steps left, right, up, or down.
  5. 'H2' pieces can move diagonally by two steps in any of the four diagonal directions.
  6. The game ends when one player captures all of the opponent's key pieces or meets the winning criteria.
